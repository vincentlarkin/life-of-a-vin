# Origin

`LifeOfAVIN-Classic` is derived from IceWM's `icedesert` theme.

- Upstream project: https://github.com/ice-wm/icewm
- Upstream source path: `lib/themes/icedesert`
- Original theme author: Nehal Mistry
- Original declared/default IceWM theme material: GPL-compatible with IceWM

The local changes keep icedesert's compact pixmap structure, 20px titlebar, and
4px frame geometry while replacing the desert beige palette with Life of a VIN
black, crimson, gold, and cream colors.
