# Origin

`LifeOfAVIN-Modern` is derived from IceWM's `CrystalBlue` theme.

- Upstream project: https://github.com/ice-wm/icewm
- Upstream source path: `lib/themes/CrystalBlue`
- Original theme author: BlueScorpio_7
- Original declared license: GPL

The local changes keep CrystalBlue's classic IceWM structure while replacing
the blue and gray palette with Life of a VIN black, crimson, gold, and cream
colors.
