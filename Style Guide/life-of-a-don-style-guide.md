# Life of a DON Style Guide

Life of a VIN is a luxury gothic interface system: black velvet surfaces,
crimson atmosphere, antique gold linework, cream typography, and hard-edged
frames. This guide records the album-facing mood in a concise, reusable form.

This is a design interpretation, not a lyric archive. Lyrics are not reproduced.

## Core Mood

Late-night luxury under pressure. The tone is expensive, private, cinematic,
and slightly dangerous: black interiors, red light through smoke, metal glints,
and cream text with ceremonial restraint.

## Design Translation

| Album motif | Theme behavior |
| --- | --- |
| Escape and late-night motion | Wide layouts, directional arrows, horizontal rules. |
| Status and control | Precise spacing, hard frames, serif display type. |
| Intoxication and blur | Crimson gradients, grain, translucent panels. |
| Desire and direct address | Focused cards, single strong actions, warm text. |
| Distrust and tension | Sharp borders, deeper blacks, rare signal red. |
| Houston night driving | Red shadow bands, low contrast backgrounds, long rails. |

Recommended balance: 68 percent black, 18 percent crimson atmosphere, 9 percent
cream text, 5 percent gold accents. Signal red appears in pinpoints only.

## Rules

- Use black and near-black as the dominant structure.
- Use crimson as atmosphere, active pressure, and danger.
- Use gold for structure: lines, icons, dividers, crests, and markers.
- Use cream for readable language.
- Keep corners square or barely eased.
- Avoid ornate corner assets until a reusable method exists.
- Avoid purple-blue cyber gradients, beige parchment, rounded SaaS cards, and
  washed-out black-on-black text.
- Do not ship album covers, artist photos, official logos, or copied lyrics.

## Type

- Display: elegant serif, optionally italic for hero moments.
- Body: readable serif with generous leading.
- Utility: mono or narrow small caps for dates, buttons, labels, and actions.

## Components

- Header: black surface, gold frame, red active nav.
- Cards: hard border, inset gold highlight, crimson shadow.
- Buttons: thin gold border, mono uppercase label, red/gold hover.
- Lists: compact rows, left date/icon, right arrow.
- Forms: black fields, gold focus, cream input text, red errors.
- Status: green only for small system success states.

## Motion

Motion should feel like light crossing a dark room: slow fades, reveal sweeps,
and subtle glow changes. Avoid bouncy, playful, or confetti-like motion.
